I grew up in a country where stray dogs were in every block of the street — it left a lasting impact on me. I have always dreamed of helping them find loving homes. If I ever reach a point in life where I can do things without worrying about money, I would want to build shelters and create a space where every dog gets a second chance. That’s why I chose to build this catalog website. It reflects a small part of that dream by displaying dogs for adoption and giving them a space to be seen. PS: All images in this project are AI-generated using Open AI's 4o mdodel. None of the dogs on the website are up for adoption.
